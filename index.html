About GasGo

GasGo Company Limited is a leading Ghanaian indigenous oil and gas company that began its operations in LPG distribution in 2006. Our headquarters are located in Accra, Ghana. We are committed to contributing to the sustainable economic and environmental development of the nation through the safe and efficient distribution of LPG.

Vision: To be an effective leader in the LPG industry in Ghana and Africa.

Mission: To contribute to the sustainable economic, environmental, and health development of Ghana through the safe distribution and use of LPG.
